Since: 2.0.0 Private New Issue

The number of headings that are supported by the theme. It's highly unlikely this will ever change - it's a web standards thing. If you just want to give your top-level headings more contrast, it's better to adjust $heading-growth-rate up, which will weigh the font size distribution more heavily towards the top.
